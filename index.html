Frequently Asked Questions

Our ETA predictions are typically accurate within 30 seconds to 2 minutes. The system uses real-time GPS data, route distance calculations, and current speed to provide the most accurate arrival times. The accuracy improves as the bus gets closer to your stop.

Yes! TrackMate is designed with network resilience in mind. The system handles socket reconnections automatically, caches data locally, and provides the last known position even during connectivity issues. The driver app also queues location updates during offline periods.

Absolutely! TrackMate supports unlimited buses per institution. Each student sees only their assigned bus, while administrators can monitor all buses on a single dashboard with fleet overview and individual bus details.

Yes, notifications are completely automatic. Students receive alerts when their bus is approaching (within 5 minutes), when it arrives at their stop, and when it departs. Users can customize notification preferences in their settings.

TrackMate is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it works directly in your browser without installation. However, you can also "Add to Home Screen" for an app-like experience with offline support and push notifications.

We take data privacy seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow FERPA guidelines for student data protection, and location data is only accessible to authorized users. No data is sold or shared with third parties.
